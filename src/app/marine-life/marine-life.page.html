Overview

The sparkling waters of the Lowcountry in South Carolina are home to a variety of marine life. The main reason for this is because of the very diverse ecosystem. A combination of areas with saltwater, freshwater, and brackish water (salt/freshwater mix) give home to a diverse community of marine life. It is not uncommon to see a group of bottlenose dolphins playing around near Fort Sumter during your visit, or racing the boat! you will definitely see a laughing gull or two (or twenty!) flying over your head. How many different Lowcountry creatures can you spot on your trip to Fort Sumter?
