Spirit of Charleston
Amenities
Boat Features

This sleek, beautiful two deck dining yacht is perfect for the Charleston Harbor. The Spirit of Charleston has an open air deck that can be fully enclosed in inclement weather. The lower deck is spacious and elegantly decorated with beautiful amber sconces for lighting and a color palette that works well with any personal style.

Weight – 85 Gross Tons

Engines – 2 John Deere #6081 300hp

Capacity – 385 passengers

Age – Built 1986

Certificates/ratings -  C.O.I. - USCG

Galley – Full sized catering galley

Environmentally – I.S.O. 14001 Certificate -EMS

AV – QSC Q-Sys and Creston integrated system control makes sending audio & video to all areas of each vessel as simple as touching a button.  Play video content locally into multiple flat screen displays on the interior decks or network video  content to the entire vessel from the wheel house via HDMI connection.  Concert quality audio systems on all decks allow for announcements and music to be heard clearly in all areas of the vessel.
